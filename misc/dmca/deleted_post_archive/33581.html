The Naruto posters shown in Naruto and Naruto Shippuden are those of Naruto movies. There is no particular "reason" behind them being there in-universe except that the anime studio wants to advertise the upcoming movies.

The one you see in the above image is Naruto Shippuden the Movie: Blood Prison.

As mentioned on the Naruto Wikia, this has happened in a few other episodes as well. Here is a list of the movies in chronological order of their release which have had their posters shown in the anime:

  • Naruto the Movie: Guardians of the Crescent Moon Kingdom

    The movie takes place after Naruto episode 196, during the original anime's filler arcs. Footage of this movie is seen in the opening and ending credits for episodes 197 through 199.

  • Road to Ninja: Naruto the Movie:

    A poster of this movie can be seen in the episode "Road to Sakura" on a fence beside a tree. Additionally, this takes place in the world that the Sakura that fell from the sky describes, even though Naruto and Sakura are transported into the Limited Tsukuyomi by Obito.
