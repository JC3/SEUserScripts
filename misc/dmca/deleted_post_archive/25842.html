The image is rather typical of an advertisement of some anime series in Japan. Searching for "Limit Break OP ED" reveals that it's the name of the third OP of Cardfight!! Vanguard. We can further confirm by comparing the blurry words in the middle of the image with the official stylized logo of Cardfight!! Vanguard:

Take note of the similarity of the word Vanguard in red

It would be great to have the original video for comparison, though tracking down the actual advertisement for comparison is quite hard at this point.

As for why an irrelevant advertisement gets in the middle of a Little Busters episode, I'm rather sure it only appears in the release by a fansub group. In the original Japanese broadcast, it's typical to have a few advertisement in the middle of the show, and whoever in charge of encoding the video failed to trim off this frame from the original transport stream.
