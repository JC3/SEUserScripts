What are these scenes in Angel Beats! parodying?

10

Angel Beats! is full of parodies, some are obvious, and some are not. The following parodies/homages have been identified:

Episode 2

  • The Laser Hallway is likely a homage to Resident Evil.

Episode 3

  • Yuri crossing her fingers (finger tenting) is a parody of the Gendo Pose from Neon Genesis Evangelion.
  • Takeyama's pi attack, having the same effect as the Ring Tightening Mantra, might be a homage to Journey to the West.
  • Takeyama's finger pointing (and appearing from behind Yuri) is a parody of Detective Conan.
  • Otonashi's first mosaic image of Angel's area is clearly Laputa: Castle in the Sky.
  • Otonashi's second mosaic image of Angel's area is clearly Howl's Moving Castle.

OVA Special: Hell's Kitchen

  • Radio communications club is ripping off from Apocalypse Now.
  • This is a wild guess, but Girls Dead Monster might actually be a homage to Dead Girls, a four-girl music group in the Red Garden OVA. Granted, it is a common team combination, but there are two more similarities between the two music groups: a former band member of GirlDeMo, Iwasawa, passed away (the second time) and left the team; a former schoolmate of the members of Dead Girls, Lise, who was murdered together with the four girls, also passed away (the second time) and left the team. Moreover, the demon, what Otonashi calls a "monster", that Hisako turned into resembles the monsters in Red Garden.

I'm still left with many scenes that I believe to be unidentified parodies. What are they parodying? Please include the episode number and a brief description so I know which one(s) you are answering, e.g. Ep 5: Angel vs SSS.

Episode 7

  • Fishing Saitou realizes they have caught Monster Stream on a hook.

Episode 10

  • Who are you imitating?

Episode 11

Episode 12

  • TK knockin' on Heaven's Door.

OVA Special: Stairway to Heaven

  • God from another anime?

  • Yuri's evil ojousama laugh.

  • Red-eyed Yui attacks from the sky with a full moon in the background.

OVA Special: Hell's Kitchen

  • Is this a Rena's death scene from Higurashi no Naku Koro ni?

The Knockin' On Heaven's Door image (Ep 12) could be a reference to the song of the same name by Bob Dylan.

I'm lead to believe this because a comment from atlantiza in this question says that TK's nonsensical English phrases are song lyrics.

On a side note, there is also a movie called "Knockin' on Heaven's Door" and the "subtitle" of the Cowboy Bebop movie was "Knockin' on Heaven's Door".

I was looking to see if the pose was a parody of anyone and discovered that the 12th episode of Angel Beats is called "Knockin' on Heaven's Door", which according to the IMDb trivia, is named after the song. So I think that the pose is all TK.

The quote:

TK: [fighting the shadow monsters] Knockin' on Heaven's Door.

Hinata: Hold on a minute. That's Bob Dylan.

Noda: But it matches the situation.
