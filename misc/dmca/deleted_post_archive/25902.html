The only way to know for sure is to contact MADHOUSE. Though it's likely that this look-alike is the product of the recycling of character styles.

The Death Note states that those who use it won't go to heaven or hell. This means that in the Death Note universe, there is heaven and hell. Strictly speaking, the idea of heaven and hell don't get along with the idea of reincarnation.

Technically, it isn't 100% impossible that the shinigami got it wrong. Perhaps they could've seen the doors, but not received an explanation, and they drew a wrong conclusion?

Summarily, it's unlikely that Light Yagami would be in this universe, but it's... possible.
