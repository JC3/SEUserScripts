Will Natsume's Book of Friends get season 5?

2

I love Natsume's Book of Friends and have been watching its anime adaptation since it was aired in 2008. The second season was aired in 2009, and then came a long wait until 2011/2012 where the third and fourth season came.

Looking from the pattern, it seems there were roughly 2-3 years between season pairs, so I thought it would came around 2014/2015... unfortunately, there was no news about it.

Is there any official news when Natsume's Book of Friends will get season 5?

Aki Tanaka

3

The first sighting appeared on Anime News Network, Natsume's Book of Friends TV Anime Gets Season 5 This Fall on 20th March 2016.

The May issue of Hakusensha's Monthly LaLa magazine is revealing on Thursday that Yuki Midorikawa's Natsume's Book of Friends (Natsume Yūjin-Chō) manga is inspiring a fifth television anime season, Natsume Yūjin-Chō Go, this fall. The issue also features a 46-page chapter of the manga with a color opening page.

And... it's confirmed on Natsume Yuujinchou's anime official site on 24th March 2016

アニメ第五期「夏目友人帳 伍」2016年秋放送決定！

アニメ最新情報は、「月刊LaLa」およびアニメ公式サイト＆Twitterにてお知らせいたします！今後の続報にご期待ください！

Anime's 5th season, "Natsume Yuujinchou Go", Fall 2016 broadcast decision!

The anime's latest information will be published on "Monthly LaLa Magazine" and also anime's official site & Twitter! Please look forward to the update!

Natsume Yuujinchou Go - Fall 2016 broadcast decision!

On 23th July 2016, the official broadcast month was released: October 2016.
