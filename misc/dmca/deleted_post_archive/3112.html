How much of the original material is animated in first season of Maoyuu Maou Yuusha?

Below are my research as I post this question.

According to Wikipedia, the original light novel series has finished serialization in 2012 with 8 volumes in total:

  • 01 “Become My property, Hero” “I Refuse!” (「この我のものとなれ、勇者よ」「断る!」)
  • 02 Conspiracy of Kurultai (忽鄰塔（クリルタイ）の陰謀)
  • 03 Expeditionary force of Seiken (聖鍵（せいけん）遠征軍)
  • 04 What Can be Done With These Hands (この手でできること)
  • Episode 01 Episode1 Female Magician from Elms country (エピソード1 楡の国の女魔法使い)
  • 05 The other side of the hill (あの丘の向こうに)
  • Episode 00 Episode0 Archer from Dune country (エピソード0 砂丘の国の弓使い)
  • Episode 02 Episode2 Female Knight from Flowers country (エピソード2 花の国の女騎士)

The 3 "episodes" are likely side stories (or prequel?) to the main story. That makes the whole of main story contained within 5 volumes.

The last episode of the anime doesn't seem to suggest that the story has ended (rather, it seems to plant seeds for future events):

The Central Nation and the Church is going to start the third Holy Crusade, and it was revealed that the Central Nation and the Church has been working with one of the Demonic Clans to keep the war going.

Maou called for meeting of all Greater Races (クリルタイ), but how the meeting plays out is not mentioned. Knowing how the expectation of the Demonic Races and Maou's philosophy to be mismatched, we can assume that the meeting has not taken place.

"クリルタイ" is mentioned in the last episode, which is part of the title of volume 2 of light novel series (忽鄰塔（クリルタイ）の陰謀). This seems to suggest that the 12 anime episodes only uses up 1 volume worth (!) of the light novel series.
